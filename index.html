Research Areas

Dynamic Spectrum Access

Advanced techniques for efficient spectrum utilization in wireless networks. I develop cognitive radio algorithms and spectrum sharing approaches that enable dynamic allocation of frequency bands based on real-time network conditions and user demands.

Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC)

Convergence of sensing and communication functionalities in next-generation wireless systems. I design unified waveforms and signal processing techniques that simultaneously enable high-speed data transmission and precise environmental sensing capabilities.

Positioning

Precise localization and positioning techniques using wireless signals. I develop advanced signal processing methods for Time-of-Arrival (ToA), Time-Difference-of-Arrival (TDoA), and Angle-of-Arrival (AoA) estimation to achieve centimeter-level accuracy in indoor and outdoor environments.

Testbeds for 5G and 6G

Development and deployment of experimental platforms for validating next-generation wireless technologies. I build open-source testbeds using OpenAirInterface and software-defined radios to prototype and evaluate 5G/6G protocols, algorithms, and network architectures.

Connected Robotics

Integration of wireless communications with robotic systems and UAV networks. I develop communication protocols and network architectures that enable seamless connectivity for autonomous vehicles, drones, and robotic swarms in mission-critical applications.

RAN Digital Twins

Digital twin technologies for Radio Access Networks enabling real-time simulation and optimization. I develop AI-driven digital replicas of RAN infrastructure that continuously synchronize with physical networks to predict performance, optimize resource allocation, and enable predictive maintenance.
